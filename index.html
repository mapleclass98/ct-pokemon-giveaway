How to Play

Landorus-Therian and Magearna are two of the most-used Pokémon in competitive Sun & Moon OU, and each can defeat the other depending on how they’re built. In this gauntlet, your goal is to play both matchups and win them both by fainting the opponent within 1 turn. Please read the steps below before you start (there is no time limit).

  1. Choose your Pokémon (the other becomes the opponent).
  2. Set your build: pick a Nature, choose a Held Item, then assign your EVs.
  3. Check your stats and the opponent's (your stats gets updated as you change your build).
  4. When you’re done building, pick your move and click Simulate Battle.
  5. If you win, switch to the other Pokémon and clear that matchup too. Your progress tracker is at the bottom of the page.

Each attempt comes with a battle log—learn from it and make appropriate changes. Have fun!
